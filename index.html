    Vision and Mission:

    The IIT Indore Alumni Association, promises a long-lasting connections between the institute and its firm, alumni base spread across the world. Commited for a vibrant, active and inclusive community, with dedicated volunteers, ammenable to attend events and diverse legacy projects. It helps preserve the institution to be as one of the world's most premier institutes. We seek innovative ways to serve our alumni and even their peers, with higher educational support, social links, professional opportunities, rock-solid network and whatever appealing to our alums.

    Priorities of Team:


  • To actively inform and involve alumni about innovative projects carried by the institute.
  • To update alumni about events, and diverse activities.
  • To foster strong bonds between our alumni, our current students and the institute, without any hurdle.
